Factorio best plugin selection

Must have plugins

  • Car/Tank/Spidertron Locator Button (Find My Car) - Where DID I leave my car/tank/spidertron/other modded vehicle? This mod adds a tool-belt icon to help you track it down.
  • Death Notice - Adds a marker to the map when a player dies so you can find the body.
  • Factorio Reach - Provides the player with a long/far reach for complete access to items, ores and trees across the entire visible play area.
    Achievable also by consol command, see https://wiki.factorio.com/Console#Enable_long_reach.
  • Squeak Through - Allows you to walk between what used to be obstacles such as directly adjacent solar panels, pipes, steam engines, mining drills, and chests. No more frustration when walking about your base!
  • Trains Switch to Manual at Temporary Stops - When a train arrives at a temporary stop, it will be switched into manual mode. If you're riding in a train and send it to a temporary stop via the map in the train interface, and you get distracted doing stuff in map view during your journey, your train will now stop where you sent it instead of continuing on its schedule.
  • VehicleSnap - Smoothly snaps movement angle when driving cars or tanks.

Highly recommended plugins

  • Afraid Of The Dark (enforced personal lights + more) - Night is too dark for you ? Enforced flashlight and personal lights, powerful balloon lights or a pair of natural tinted night glasses could be the solution !
  • Automatic Train Painter - Automatically colors locomotives and wagons based on their contents.
  • Bottleneck - A tool for locating input starved machines.
  • Even Distribution - Modifies CTRL + Click Drag to evenly distribute the items over multiple buildings. Also adds Inventory Cleanup hotkey (SHIFT+C) which evenly distributes unneeded items from inventory into nearby machines.
  • More Minimap Autohide - Hides the minimap when hovering over certain entities so that the side-panel doesn't disappear off the edge of the screen.
  • Pump Anywhere - Pump anywhere allows offshore pumps to be placed anywhere. This allows the creation of water well pumps without the use of landfill.
    Alternative mod is Waterfill which allows the placement of water just like landfill.

Planning and Blueprinting support plugins

  • Rate Calculator - Select an area to view maximum production and consumption rates for materials, electricity, pollution, and heat. Similar to Max Rate Calculator.
  • Editor Extensions - Extends the map editor with new features and tools, makes tweaks to it to facilitate easier sandbox play, and adds items and entities to assist with testing factories. The successor to Infinity Mode, which was inspired by Creative Mode.
